01 // The Challenge

A service business invisible online

Simca Agencies ran a thriving cleaning company across Kenya with no digital presence — relying entirely on phone calls and word-of-mouth to reach customers.

Zero digital footprint

No website, no online service catalog, and no way for potential customers to discover the company’s 15+ professional cleaning services or 5 locations across Kenya.

Manual lead capture

Every inquiry came through phone calls with no structured way to qualify leads, capture customer information, or follow up systematically.

Disconnected presence

Service catalog, pricing, locations, and company credentials were scattered across disconnected WhatsApp messages, print brochures, and email threads.

02 // The Solution

Modern Next.js platform built for growth

A comprehensive 20+ page corporate website built with Next.js 15 App Router, featuring interactive maps with Leaflet, a booking form powered by Resend email integration, dark mode, a photo gallery with lightbox, a GEO-optimized blog, and extensive JSON-LD structured data for AI search visibility. The platform transforms phone-based inquiries into a streamlined digital experience where customers browse services, view locations, and book directly.

03 // Key Features

Full-service digital platform

Every component designed to showcase services, capture leads, and build trust with potential clients.

Interactive Service Catalog

20+ individual service pages covering 5 core service lines from contract cleaning to landscaping, each with detailed descriptions, icons, and prominent call-to-action buttons.

→

Smart Booking System

Full booking form with service selection dropdown (with icons), location picker across 5 Kenyan cities, preferred date picker, and email notification via Resend API to both the owner and customer.

→

WhatsApp Integration

Floating WhatsApp widget with pulse animation linking directly to the sales line, sticky mobile CTA bar with call and quote buttons appearing after scroll, and WhatsApp webhook for booking notifications.

→

Dark Mode & Accessibility

Full dark mode via next-themes with CSS variable theming, ARIA labels throughout, prefers-reduced-motion support, semantic HTML structure, and keyboard-navigable interactive elements.

→

Interactive Maps

Dual map system: Leaflet with OpenStreetMap and Nominatim geocoding for all 5 locations with custom markers, plus a Google Maps embed for the Mombasa headquarters with branded styling.

→

SEO & GEO Optimization

Extensive JSON-LD structured data for Organization, LocalBusiness, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb, and Article schemas. Dynamic sitemap, robots.txt, Open Graph tags, and meta keywords for maximum search visibility.
